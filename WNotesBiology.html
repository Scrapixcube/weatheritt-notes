Biology Notes

Cell Structure:

Animal cell:

Nucleus: controls(some) cell activitys. contains DNA.

Mitochondria: site of aerobic respiration.

Ribosomes: site of protien synthesis.

Cytoplasm: site of chemical reactions.

Cell Membrane: phospholipid bi-layer. allows(some) materials into and out of the cell.

Plant cell:

Nucleus: controls(some) cell activitys. contains DNA.

Mitochondria: site of aerobic respiration.

Ribosomes: site of protien synthesis.

Cytoplasm: site of chemical reactions.

Cell Membrane: phospholipid bi-layer. allows(some) materials into and out of the cell.

Cell Wall: provides structure and shape. made of cellulose. fully permeable.

Vacuole: provides support, regulates water level and holds cell sap.

Chloroplast: site of photosynthesis.

Fungus cell:

Nucleus: controls(some) cell activitys. contains DNA.

Mitochondria: site of aerobic respiration.

Ribosomes: site of protien synthesis.

Cytoplasm: site of chemical reactions.

Cell Membrane: phospholipid bi-layer. allows(some) materials into and out of the cell.

Cell Wall: provides structure and shape. not made of cellulose. fully permeable.

Vacuole: provides support, regulates water level and holds cell sap.

Bacteria cell:

Ribosomes: site of protien synthesis.

Cytoplasm: site of chemical reactions.

Cell Membrane: phospholipid bi-layer. allows(some) materials into and out of the cell.

Cell Wall: provides structure and shape. not made of cellulose. fully permeable.

Plasmid: circular ring of DNA.
